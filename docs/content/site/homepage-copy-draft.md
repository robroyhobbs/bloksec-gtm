## Section: Why this matters now
Passwords and phone-dependent MFA break down where real work happens: on the floor, at the register, across shared stations, and during shift changes.

That creates the same pattern again and again:
- workers share credentials because speed matters
- password resets and lockouts slow operations
- compliance gets harder to defend
- IT teams spend time fixing access instead of reducing risk

## Section: One platform for two workforce realities
### For workers with devices
BlokKey delivers mobile-first passwordless authentication for office and knowledge workers.

### For workers without them
BlokBadge gives frontline teams fast, secure authentication on shared devices without relying on employee smartphones.

### Unifying line
One platform, one workforce story, fewer gaps.

## Section: Outcomes buyers care about
### Reduce reset and lockout burden
Eliminate a major source of help-desk friction and give teams a cleaner way to handle onboarding, offboarding, and re-provisioning.

### Make shared-device access accountable
Replace shared-credential workarounds with per-worker authentication that is easier to audit and easier to manage.

### Move faster in frontline workflows
Support worker switching, shift changes, and high-turnover environments without dragging operations back into password chaos.

### Strengthen your security posture
Use phishing-resistant, passwordless authentication that fits the realities of mixed workforces instead of assuming every worker looks like a desk worker.
