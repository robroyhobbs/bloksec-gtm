# Live Site Fixes

## Conversion issues
### High priority
- `demo` appears to resolve to a generic contact/newsletter page rather than a focused conversion page.
- `free-trial` appears to resolve to the same generic experience and does not explain the offer.
- `contact` and `legal` also appear to collapse into the same generic layout, which reduces trust.
- The homepage does not clearly tell visitors what they should do next.

### Recommended fixes
- Build a real demo page with audience-specific paths and expectation setting.
- Decide whether `free trial` is a real product offer, a guided trial, or a placeholder. Then rewrite accordingly.
- Make `contact` a true contact page and keep `legal` separate from lead-generation content.
- Align CTAs across product and case-study pages.

## Messaging issues
### High priority
- Homepage does not lead with the sharpest wedge: frontline/shared-device access.
- SMB and mid-market fit is not explicit enough.
- The split between office-worker and frontline-worker value is visible but not framed as one coherent buying story.
- The site underplays password-reset elimination, worker switching, and fast re-provisioning after lost credentials.

### Recommended fixes
- Rewrite the hero around frontline and shared-device pain.
- Add a short "who this is for" block near the top.
- Add clearer language around lost-password elimination and instant recovery alternatives.
- Add stronger stack-fit messaging with Microsoft, Google Workspace, Okta, and others.

## Product-page issues
### BlokBadge
- Contains a copy bug: `With BlokKey` appears inside the BlokBadge comparison section.
- Should lead more clearly with phone-free authentication and shift-based/shared-device workflows.
- Needs stronger language around badge re-issue, rapid onboarding, and worker turnover.

### BlokKey
- Stronger than before, but still leans heavily on security language over buyer workflow outcomes.
- Could better connect mobile-first authentication to mixed workforces and partner-led deployments.

## Proof issues
- Case studies exist but need stronger vertical framing.
- Outcomes should be easier to scan.
- The site should surface product maturity signals like v4.0 reporting, templates, and docs more clearly.

## Trust and clarity issues
- Generic contact-page reuse across multiple URLs creates avoidable doubt.
- Any broken or duplicated surface on a security product site damages credibility faster than on a typical B2B site.
- Technical terms should be translated into buyer outcomes more consistently.

## Recommended execution order
1. Fix broken or generic conversion pages.
2. Rewrite homepage hero and top narrative.
3. Repair BlokBadge page and strengthen the frontline workflow story.
4. Reframe case studies by industry and outcomes.
5. Add clearer proof around reporting, onboarding, and integration fit.
