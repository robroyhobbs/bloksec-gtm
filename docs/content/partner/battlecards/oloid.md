# Battlecard — OLOID

## Why OLOID matters
OLOID is one of the closest frontline-oriented competitors and carries stronger enterprise-logo proof than BlokSec today.

## What OLOID wins on
- visible traction in frontline-heavy enterprise environments
- multiple frontline authentication modalities
- stronger enterprise-market recognition in this specific wedge

## Where BlokSec can win
- SMB and mid-market positioning
- partner and MSP-led motion
- a simpler story around rapid issuance, replacement, and operational fit
- a broader `office + frontline` platform story when both groups need coverage

## How to position
Avoid overclaiming that OLOID always requires infrastructure unless the exact scenario is known.

Safer framing:
- `BlokSec is designed to lower friction for mid-market and partner-led deployments.`
- `BlokSec tells a simpler badge-and-workflow story for buyers who need practical rollout more than enterprise platform breadth.`
- `BlokSec covers both frontline and office-worker authentication paths in one narrative.`

## Sales talk track
`OLOID is a serious frontline player, especially in larger enterprise environments. BlokSec is strongest when the buyer needs a simpler, partner-friendly path into frontline authentication without turning the project into a large enterprise transformation.`

## Proof still needed
- clearer deployment comparison
- pricing and implementation contrast where public evidence exists
- stronger BlokSec proof in manufacturing, logistics, or similar frontline use cases
