# Battlecard — Secret Double Octopus

## Why SDO matters
Secret Double Octopus is relevant because it has a real passwordless story and a visible MSP/channel posture.

## What SDO wins on
- stronger existing passwordless brand awareness than many smaller vendors
- a formalized MSP program story
- broad enterprise passwordless credibility

## Where BlokSec can win
- a sharper frontline/shared-device wedge
- simpler storytelling for environments where phone-based or device-heavy models break down
- partner conversations centered on SMB and mid-market operational pain instead of generic passwordless transformation

## How to position
Do not dismiss SDO as niche or low-awareness without updated evidence.

Lead with:
- BlokSec is strongest where frontline workflows are the deciding factor.
- The story is not just `passwordless`, but `passwordless for workers traditional models overlook`.
- BlokSec can be framed around workflow pain, shared devices, and worker switching rather than broad category messaging.

## Sales talk track
`SDO belongs in the shortlist when the buyer wants an established passwordless conversation. BlokSec stands out when the real problem is frontline workflow friction, shared-device accountability, and fast worker access in environments where traditional models are awkward.`

## Proof still needed
- updated channel comparison
- partner-program comparison
- clearer evidence that BlokSec can win in frontline-first opportunities, not just category discussions
