# Battlecard — Cisco Duo

## Why Duo matters
Duo is familiar, trusted, channel-friendly, and often already present in SMB and mid-market conversations.

## What Duo wins on
- strong brand recognition
- established channel motion
- broad familiarity with IT teams and MSPs
- easy entry point for MFA conversations

## Where BlokSec can win
- frontline and shared-device environments where phone-centric MFA creates friction
- buyers who want a stronger passwordless story rather than a safer version of MFA
- teams struggling with reset burden, worker switching, and non-desk workflows

## How to position
Lead with workflow fit, not abstract crypto.

Use lines like:
- `Duo is often a good answer for workers with devices. BlokSec is strongest where device assumptions break down.`
- `BlokSec is about removing password and shared-device friction, not just adding another approval step.`

## Sales talk track
`If every worker has a reliable device and the goal is to layer on MFA, Duo is a natural benchmark. If workers share devices, move across shifts, or cannot rely on phones, BlokSec becomes a stronger operational fit.`

## Proof still needed
- clearer before-and-after workflow comparisons
- stronger public case studies in frontline-heavy environments
- a concise comparison of reset burden and worker switching experience
