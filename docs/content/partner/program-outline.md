# Partner Program Outline

## Purpose
Give MSPs and channel partners a simple way to understand where BlokSec fits, how the economics work, and what support they should expect.

## Who this is for
- MSPs serving SMB and mid-market clients
- partners already managing Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, or mixed identity estates
- firms with clients in frontline-heavy industries

## Core partner value
- fewer password-reset tickets and related support burden
- a sellable recurring security line item
- a stronger compliance story for shared-device environments
- an angle that reaches workers traditional phone-based MFA does not serve well

## Proposed program shape
### Referral
- easiest entry point
- suited for firms validating demand
- compensation through referral fee

### Reseller
- better margin for partners actively selling and supporting deployments
- includes enablement, collateral, and basic co-marketing support

### Strategic
- for partners with repeatable volume or deeper integration needs
- includes stronger technical support, planning access, and white-label options if offered

## Assets partners need
- one clear tier model
- NFR or trial access
- ROI calculator
- short partner pitch deck
- vertical-specific outreach sequences
- competitive responses for Entra, Duo, OLOID, and SDO

## Immediate recommendation
Keep the initial program simple and operationally realistic. The near-term goal is to remove friction for first partners, not to build a complex channel bureaucracy.
