# MSP List Building & Outreach Optimization Playbook

## Overview

Outreach quality depends entirely on list quality. This playbook covers how to build targeted MSP prospect lists for manufacturing and retail verticals, and how to optimize outreach for higher response rates.

---

## Part 1: List Building

### Where to Find Manufacturing & Retail-Focused MSPs

#### 1. LinkedIn Sales Navigator (Primary)

**Search filters:**
- **Job Title:** "Managed Services" OR "MSP" OR "IT Services"
- **Industry:** Information Technology & Services
- **Company Size:** 11-200 employees (sweet spot for MSP partners)
- **Keywords in profile/company:** "manufacturing" OR "retail" OR "compliance" OR "PCI" OR "HIPAA"

**Advanced search strings:**
```
"managed service provider" AND ("manufacturing" OR "factory" OR "plant")
"MSP" AND ("retail" OR "POS" OR "PCI DSS")
"IT services" AND ("compliance" OR "SOC 2" OR "audit")
```

**What to look for:**
- MSPs that mention specific verticals on their website/profile
- MSPs that list compliance services (PCI, HIPAA, SOC 2)
- MSPs that partner with Microsoft, ConnectWise, or Datto

**Enrichment:** Once you find prospects on LinkedIn, use Apollo.io or Hunter.io to get verified email addresses.

#### 7. LinkedIn Content Engagement Mining

**Method:** Find MSPs through their content engagement, not just their profiles.

1. Search LinkedIn for posts about: "PCI DSS compliance", "manufacturing cybersecurity", "retail data breach", "shared workstation security"
2. Look at who commented/liked/shared these posts
3. Filter for MSP owners, sales leaders, or vCISOs at IT services companies
4. These are *warm* prospects — they've already signaled interest in the topic

**Tool:** Use LinkedIn's activity feed or a tool like Taplio to track engagement on relevant topics.

## Part 2: Outreach Optimization

### Channel Mix (Don't Rely on Email Alone)

| Channel | Purpose | When |
|---------|---------|------|
| **Email** | Primary cold outreach | Day 0, 3, 7 |
| **LinkedIn Connect** | Warm the relationship | Day 1 |
| **LinkedIn DM** | Casual follow-up | Day 10 |
| **LinkedIn Engagement** | Build familiarity | Day 5 (like/comment on their posts) |
| **Phone** | For high-priority prospects | After email open / click |
| **Video (Loom)** | Stand out from text-only | Use in Email 2 or 3 |

### Subject Line Testing

Test these subject line patterns (track open rates, aim for 30%+):

**Pattern 1: Question**
- "Your manufacturing clients still using passwords on the floor?"
- "How are your retail clients handling PCI DSS 4.0 MFA?"

**Pattern 2: Stat-led**
- "40% of your help desk tickets have a $0 solution"
- "$70 per password reset × 200 workers = why we built this"

**Pattern 3: Compliance urgency**
- "PCI DSS 4.0 + your retail clients = MFA gap?"
- "FDA 21 CFR Part 11 — are your pharma clients covered?"

**Pattern 4: Peer reference**
- "How other MSPs are adding $4,800/yr per manufacturing client"
- "The MFA product manufacturing MSPs are adding to their stack"

### Timing Optimization

| Factor | Best Practice |
|--------|-------------|
| **Day of week** | Tuesday-Thursday (avoid Monday inbox clutter, Friday wind-down) |
| **Time of day** | 8:00-9:30 AM or 1:00-2:00 PM recipient's local time |
| **Sequence spacing** | Day 0, Day 3, Day 7, Day 10, Day 14 |
| **Follow-up limit** | Max 5 touches, then pause for 30 days |
| **Re-engage** | After 30 days with new content (breach analysis, compliance guide) |

### Personalization Framework

Move beyond `{FirstName}`. Personalize on these dimensions:

| Level | What to Personalize | Example |
|-------|-------------------|---------|
| **Basic** | Name, company | "Hi {FirstName}, I noticed {Company}..." |
| **Medium** | Industry focus | "...works with manufacturing clients..." |
| **High** | Specific trigger | "...saw your post about PCI compliance..." |
| **Highest** | Client-specific | "...companies like your client {ClientName} often deal with..." |

**Rule:** At minimum, Level 2 (industry focus) for every email. Level 3+ for high-priority prospects.

### Response Handling

| Response Type | Action |
|--------------|--------|
| **Positive / Interested** | Book demo within 48 hours. Send calendar link immediately. |
| **"Not now"** | Add to quarterly nurture. Send monthly content (breach analyses). |
| **"Tell me more"** | Send MSP partner one-pager PDF + ROI calculator link. Follow up in 3 days. |
| **"Not interested"** | Respect it. Remove from sequence. Note reason if given. |
| **Auto-reply / OOO** | Resend 3 days after their return date. |
| **No response (all 5 emails)** | Pause 30 days. Re-engage with new angle or content. |

### Tracking & Metrics

Track these weekly:

| Metric | Target | Tool |
|--------|--------|------|
| Emails sent | 20-30/week | CRM or outreach tool |
| Open rate | 30%+ | Email tracking |
| Reply rate | 5-10% | Email tracking |
| Meetings booked | 2-3/week | Calendar |
| Demo-to-partner conversion | 20%+ | CRM |

**Tools to consider:**
- **Apollo.io** — List building + email sequences + tracking (free tier available)
- **Instantly.ai** — Email warmup + outreach automation
- **Lemlist** — Personalized email sequences with tracking
- **HubSpot CRM** — Free CRM for pipeline tracking
- **Calendly** — Meeting scheduling link

## Part 3: Quick-Start Checklist

### Week 1: Build Your First List

- [ ] Set up Apollo.io or Hunter.io account (free tier)
- [ ] Run LinkedIn Sales Navigator search for manufacturing MSPs (save 25 prospects)
- [ ] Run LinkedIn Sales Navigator search for retail MSPs (save 25 prospects)
- [ ] Check ChannelE2E Top 250 and CRN MSP 500 for vertical-focused MSPs
- [ ] Enrich top 20 prospects with verified email addresses
- [ ] Score all 20 using the qualification criteria above
- [ ] Prioritize top 10 for outreach

### Week 2: Launch First Outreach

- [ ] Set up email tracking (Apollo, Lemlist, or Instantly)
- [ ] Send Email 1 to top 5 manufacturing MSP prospects
- [ ] Send Email 1 to top 5 retail MSP prospects
- [ ] Connect with all 10 on LinkedIn (use connection message templates)
- [ ] Track opens and clicks daily

### Week 3-4: Iterate

- [ ] Send Email 2 to non-responders (Day 3)
- [ ] Engage with prospects' LinkedIn posts (like/comment)
- [ ] Send Email 3 to non-responders (Day 7)
- [ ] Analyze: Which subject lines got opens? Which verticals responded?
- [ ] Build next batch of 20 prospects based on what's working
- [ ] Book first demo calls
