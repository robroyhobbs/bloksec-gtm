## Recommended BlokSec Pricing

| Product | Price | Notes |

Volume discounts at 250, 500, 1,000+ users.

### MSP / Partner Pricing
| Tier | Discount off List | MSP Margin |
|------|-------------------|-----------|
| Referral | 20% referral fee | — |
| Reseller (25+ seats) | 40% wholesale | 40% |
| Strategic (100+ seats) | 45-55% wholesale | 45-55% |

**Example (Reseller at $5 list):**
- MSP buys at $3.00/user/mo
- MSP sells at $5.00/user/mo
- MSP margin: $2.00/user/mo
- At 500 users: $1,000/month recurring margin
