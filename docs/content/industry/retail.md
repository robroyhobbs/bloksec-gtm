# Retail

## Why this vertical fits
Retail combines shared POS devices, high turnover, seasonal onboarding, and strong PCI pressure.

## Core pain
- multiple workers share registers and back-office terminals
- seasonal or part-time workers need fast provisioning
- password resets waste store and help-desk time
- managers cannot afford login friction during peak traffic

## Why passwords fail here
Passwords and traditional MFA create a constant tension between speed and accountability. Shared credentials are convenient but undermine PCI controls and traceability.

## Why BlokSec matters here
- fast sign-in for shared terminals
- unique worker authentication without assuming a company phone
- better onboarding for seasonal staff
- easier replacement path when a credential is lost or a worker leaves

## GTM angle
Lead with PCI DSS 4.0 plus high-turnover operations:
- unique authentication for shared POS environments
- less reset burden for distributed teams
- faster onboarding and offboarding

## Best supporting assets
- PCI-focused compliance material
- ROI calculator
- partner one-pager
- retail MSP outreach sequence
