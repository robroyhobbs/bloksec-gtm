# Warehousing and Logistics

## Why this vertical fits
Warehousing and logistics environments depend on shared terminals, scanners, shift-based labor, and constant movement.

## Core pain
- shared devices across zones and shifts
- gloves, cold environments, or mobile workflows reduce usability
- seasonal surges create constant onboarding pressure
- remote or distributed facilities increase help-desk friction

## Why passwords fail here
Passwords are hard to use in cold or gloved conditions and create avoidable delay at shift changes and shared-device handoff moments.

## Why BlokSec matters here
- simpler shared-device authentication
- better fit for fast worker turnover
- less dependence on employee phones
- faster recovery when a worker loses access or needs re-issuance

## GTM angle
Lead with throughput and worker switching:
- speed at shift change
- cleaner accountability on shared devices
- lower reset burden in distributed operations

- frontline worker market research
