# Go-to-Market Plan

## Strategy Summary

Capture the underserved frontline worker authentication market through MSP/partner channel, targeting manufacturing and retail verticals where compliance mandates are creating urgent buyer demand.

## Phase 1: Validate (Weeks 1-2)
1. Identify 10 manufacturing/retail-focused MSPs
2. Send 5 outreach emails to test messaging
3. Publish MSP partner program page
4. Launch ROI calculator on website
5. Post 3x on LinkedIn with compliance-angle content

## Phase 2: Build Foundation (Month 1)
1. Formalize 3-tier MSP partner program
2. Publish compliance guides (PCI DSS 4.0, FDA 21 CFR Part 11)
3. Start weekly breach analysis blog series
4. Create MSP sales enablement kit
5. Sign first 2-3 MSP referral partners

## Phase 3: Scale (Months 2-3)
1. Get 2-3 pilot customer case studies
2. Launch industry landing pages (/manufacturing, /retail)
3. Build competitive battlecards for partners
4. Explore TD Synnex distribution partnership
5. Begin guest posting (Dark Reading, CSO Online)

## Phase 4: Expand (Months 4-6)
1. Attend MSP Summit / Channel Partners Conference
2. Pursue Microsoft co-sell partnership
3. Build breach database as content asset
4. Evaluate healthcare market entry timing

## Success Metrics
- Month 1: 5 MSP conversations, 2 referral partners signed
- Month 3: 10 MSP partners, 500 users deployed, 3 case studies
- Month 6: 25 MSP partners, 2,000 users, $7K MRR
- Year 1: 10 active MSP partners, 2,000 users, $84K ARR
