# MSP / Partner Channel Strategy

## Why MSPs Are the Right Channel

BlokSec's sweet spot: SMB-to-mid-market companies with frontline workers who need MFA compliance but lack in-house IAM teams. That's the MSP customer profile.

## Value Proposition to MSPs

1. **Reduce their own costs** — Fewer password reset tickets (40% of all tickets)
2. **New recurring revenue** — $3-5/user/mo managed service
3. **Compliance differentiator** — "We make you PCI/FDA compliant" upsell
4. **Frontline coverage** — Something Okta/Microsoft/Duo can't do

## Partner Program Tiers

| Tier | Requirements | Margin | Support |
|------|-------------|--------|---------|
| Referral | Sign up, complete training | 20% referral fee | Sales collateral |
| Reseller | 25+ seats, 3+ clients | 40% | + Partner portal, co-marketing |
| Strategic | 100+ seats, 10+ clients | 55% | + Dedicated SE, white-label option |

## Target MSP Profile

**Ideal partner:**
- 200-2,000 managed seats
- Serves manufacturing SMBs, retail chains, or food processing
- Already selling M365 or Google Workspace
- Has compliance-driven clients (PCI DSS, FDA, SOC 2)
- Currently struggling with password reset ticket volume

**Where to find them:**
- ConnectWise, Datto, Kaseya partner ecosystems
- CompTIA community
- TD Synnex distribution (follow IDEE's model)
- MSP Summit / Channel Partners Conference
- Local IT peer groups (HTG, ASCII, Robin Robins)
- LinkedIn: "MSP" + "manufacturing" or "retail" search
- Reddit: r/msp community

## MSP Economics Example

**200-seat manufacturing client:**
- List price: $5/user/mo = $1,000/mo client revenue
- MSP cost (Reseller tier): $3/user/mo = $600/mo
- MSP margin: $400/mo = $4,800/yr per client
- Password reset savings for MSP: ~200 tickets/yr × $70 = $14,000 saved
- Total MSP value: $18,800/yr per client
