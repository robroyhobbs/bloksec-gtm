Why this matters now

Security teams have plenty of MFA options for workers with assigned devices. The harder problem is the one traditional approaches still handle poorly: workers on shared stations, in shift-based environments, with phones that cannot be assumed and passwords that create daily friction.

Workflow friction

Password resets and lockouts waste time

Help-desk burden, lost productivity, and repeated access friction pile up fast in high-turnover and shared-device environments.

Shared-device risk

Shared credentials weaken accountability

Teams under pressure often trade secure behavior for speed when the login experience does not fit how work actually happens.

Compliance pressure

Audit expectations keep getting stronger

Retail, regulated operations, logistics, and care environments need cleaner user-level accountability without making work harder.

One platform for two workforce realities

Workers without devices

BlokBadge for frontline and shared-device teams

Fast authentication for workers on shared devices or in environments where employee smartphones cannot be assumed.
