Why this gets attention

The issue is not just passwords. It is what passwords and phone-dependent MFA force teams to do when the work happens on shared stations, under time pressure, and across constant worker change.

Reset pressure

Lockouts waste time fast

Reset-heavy access operations create repeated friction for workers and a steady support burden for IT.

Workflow mismatch

Shared devices break desk-first assumptions

When access is designed for personal devices and static desks, frontline teams absorb the workaround cost.

Accountability

Shared credentials weaken traceability

High-turnover and regulated environments need better user-level accountability without adding more friction.
