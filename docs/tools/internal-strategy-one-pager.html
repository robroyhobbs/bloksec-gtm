Strategy Pillars

MSP / Partner Channel

  • Primary GTM route — MSPs serve the exact SMB/mid-market profile that needs us
  • 40-55% margins for partners on $3-5/user/mo recurring revenue
  • Eliminates 30-40% of MSP help desk tickets (password resets at $70 each)
  • 3-tier program: Referral (20%) → Reseller (40%) → Strategic (55%)
  • Force multiplier — partners sell, deploy, and support

Content & SEO Engine

  • Weekly: Breach analysis series — newsjack breaches, show passwordless prevention
  • Monthly: Compliance deep-dives — PCI DSS 4.0, FDA 21 CFR Part 11, SOC 2
  • Quarterly: Industry landing pages — manufacturing, retail, warehousing
  • Own "frontline worker authentication" — no competitor has claimed this space
  • ~6-8 hours/week for all content production

Why We Win
  • vs. Duo/Okta/Entra: They require smartphones. BlokBadge doesn't.
  • vs. OLOID: OLOID needs RFID/NFC infrastructure. We need a printer.
  • vs. Yubico: Keys cost $25-90 each, get lost. Badges are printed.
  • vs. HYPR: Enterprise-complex. We're built for MSPs and mid-market.
  • Full coverage: BlokKey (office) + BlokBadge (floor) = one platform, every worker
