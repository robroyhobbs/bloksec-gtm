# PRODUCT

## Product
BlokSec is a passwordless authentication platform for organizations that need secure, phishing-resistant access across both office workers and frontline/shared-device workers.

## Users
- IT and security leaders at SMB and mid-market organizations
- MSPs and channel partners serving frontline-heavy clients
- Operations and compliance leaders in regulated or high-turnover environments

## Core problem
Traditional MFA and password-based access break down in frontline environments where workers share devices, cannot use phones, wear PPE, move between shifts, and constantly trigger resets, lockouts, and compliance gaps.

## Core value
BlokSec gives organizations one path to secure access across office and frontline workers while reducing password-reset burden, improving auditability, and supporting faster onboarding and re-provisioning.

## Best-fit ICP
- SMB and mid-market organizations
- Frontline-heavy or shared-device environments
- Industries where fast secure authentication and painless credential recovery alternatives matter most
- Initial verticals: manufacturing, retail, warehousing/logistics, food processing, and care settings

## Channel strategy
MSPs and partners are the primary scale channel for the initial motion.
